Feedback Response (Project Proposal)¶

We received the following feedback and have highlighted our response to some of your concerns. Do get back to us with any additional follow up questions or suggestions or if you think anything is inaccurate or inadequate.

I am not sure if your classification problem is appropriately set up. When you plan to learn the value of a player, what is your timeframe. A player might be initially an average player, then after a couple of weeks become a budding talent, and then again become an average player. What is your plan to model the temporal change of the value of a player? (2) Who is the audience of your project? Is there any business that benefits from your paper? How? (3) What is a potential way to incorporate the budget constraint in a supervised learning problem? Do you have any specific candidate model that can handle such a constraint?

FIFA stats are updated real time. We use this dataset taken at the beginning of the 2019 season. The value of a player is decided by the team he has a contract with. His value might vary based on his performance during the course of the season but that data would be updated on FIFA as well. We would just have to have captured data at a specific point and look at his statistics to make decisions with a model using that data instance.

FIFA data is based on real players with real time updates. The audience of our project could vary from simple players of the FIFA console/PC games to actual football managers or even fantasy football players (By football here I mean soccer). The business benefits could be to actually sell a detailed system to football clubs to use for various statistical analyses and finding replacements for players on their teams, etc.

We want to try and use clustering algorithms like KNN to find like for like replacements for players based on budget constraints. We will hard code the constraints to find only those points which match our requirements.

Motivation for your problem¶

We as sports enthusiasts follow soccer and play FIFA a lot ourselves. Thinking of the possibility of integrating soccer into our course seems exciting by itself but the possibilities in addition to that, like picking like for like replacements for a team, building a team of the best 11 with a specific budget, having a significant advantage while playing manager mode on FIFA with statistical knowledge, etc

Although were not going to incorporate all these problems in our project, we hope we are allowed to present flexible outcomes based on trial and error and feasibility.

def wageConverter(val):
    if val[-1] == 'M':
        val = val[1:-1]
        val = float(val) * 1000000
        return val
        
    elif val[-1] == 'K':
        val = val[1:-1]
        val = float(val) * 1000
        return val
    
    else:
        return 0

    
fifa['Value in Pounds'] = fifa['Value'].apply(wageConverter)
fifa['Wage in Pounds'] = fifa['Wage'].apply(wageConverter)

fifa.drop(columns=['Value', 'Wage'], inplace=True)
